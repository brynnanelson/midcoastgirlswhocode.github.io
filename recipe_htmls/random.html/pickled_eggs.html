Pickled Eggs

Ingredients


12 eggs
6 red beets, or moreif desired (reserve juice)
Salt, pepper
1 cup vinegar

Instructions

1) Hard boil eggs
2) Boil several red beets, adding 1/2 teaspoon salt and 1/2 teaspoon pepper
3) Drain juice into large jar and cool
4)Add vinegar and season to taste with salt and pepper
5) Place hard boiled eggs in and let stand for an hour before serving

Makes 12 servings
